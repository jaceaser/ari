title: Joint Tenancy
type: glossary
definition: A form of co-ownership where two or more people each hold an equal undivided interest with the right of survivorship.
plainEnglish: "All owners have equal shares and when one dies, their share automatically goes to the surviving owners — NOT to their heirs. Opposite of tenancy in common."
relatedTerms: [tenancy-in-common, right-of-survivorship]
tags: [ownership, co-ownership]
summary: Co-ownership with automatic survivorship — prevents the heir fractures that occur with tenancy in common.
aliases: [joint ownership, JTWROS, joint tenants]
searchTerms: [joint tenancy, joint ownership, survivorship, JTWROS]
