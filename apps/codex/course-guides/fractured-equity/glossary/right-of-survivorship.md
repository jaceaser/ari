title: Right of Survivorship
type: glossary
definition: A feature of joint tenancy ownership where a deceased co-owner's interest automatically transfers to the surviving co-owners rather than to the deceased's heirs.
plainEnglish: "With joint tenancy + right of survivorship, the last survivor ends up owning everything. Without it (tenancy in common), each owner's share goes to their own heirs."
relatedTerms: [joint-tenancy, tenancy-in-common, intestate-succession]
tags: [ownership, co-ownership, inheritance]
summary: The automatic inheritance feature of joint tenancy — absent in tenancy in common, which is why TIC properties generate heir fractures.
aliases: [survivorship rights, JTWROS]
searchTerms: [right of survivorship, JTWROS, joint tenancy survivorship, automatic inheritance]
