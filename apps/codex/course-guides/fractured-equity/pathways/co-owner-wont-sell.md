entryCondition: You hold a recorded partial interest in a property. One or more co-owners refuse to sell, negotiate, or respond.
tags: [hostile-cotenants, partition, leverage]
aliases: [hostile co-owner, uncooperative heir, hostile partition pathway]

prerequisites: []
searchTerms: [co-owner won't sell, hostile heir, uncooperative co-owner, partition pathway]
featured: true
steps:
  - order: 1
    topicSlug: partial-interest
    label: Document your ownership
    decisionPoints:
      - Is your deed properly recorded?
      - Do you have a clear title insurance policy on your interest?
    risks:
      - Unrecorded interests are vulnerable to third-party purchasers
  - order: 2
    topicSlug: contribution-suit
    label: Calculate and demand contribution
    decisionPoints:
      - Have you paid taxes, insurance, or repairs beyond your share?
      - Are the amounts material enough to justify a demand letter?
    risks:
      - Small contribution amounts may not justify litigation costs
  - order: 3
    topicSlug: contribution-suit
    label: File contribution suit if demand ignored
    decisionPoints:
      - Did the co-owner respond to the demand letter?
      - Is the co-owner judgment-proof?
  - order: 4
    topicSlug: partition-action
    label: File partition action
    decisionPoints:
      - Can the property be physically divided (partition in kind)?
      - Is partition by sale more appropriate?
    risks:
      - Court-ordered partition sale may be below market
      - Timeline 9-18 months
  - order: 5
    topicSlug: partition-action
    label: Bid at commissioner's sale or negotiate settlement
    decisionPoints:
      - Has the filing prompted the co-owner to negotiate?
      - Do you have funds to bid at the partition sale?
